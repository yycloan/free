1 Voucher
Redeem your voucher and then enter your ID number and click "Improve My Credit" to get your loan released instantly.
⚙️ How to Pay the R50 Clearance Fee (Self-Service):

• Click on any Credit Score Improvement method
• Choose your voucher type
• Enter your voucher PIN (from the till slip or app)
• The system will clear your record and trigger your loan payout
ℹ️ You can buy a 1Voucher using airtime by following a few simple steps, either online or at participating retailers:

1. Find a Retailer: Locate a retailer that sells 1Vouchers. You can purchase them from stores like Flash, PEP, or Shoprite, or buy directly online from the 1Voucher website.
2. Choose the Voucher Amount: Pick the amount that suits your needs. 1Vouchers are available in different values, for use in shopping, gaming, or online services.
3. Pay Using Airtime: Online purchases allow you to pay with mobile airtime. For in-store, use cash or airtime (if accepted by the store).
4. Receive Your Voucher: After payment, you'll get a 16-digit PIN. This code is used to redeem the voucher for services or products.
