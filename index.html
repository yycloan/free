Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our loan services

How do I apply for a loan?

Simply click on "Apply Now" and complete our secure online application form. You'll need basic personal information, employment details, and banking information. The process takes just a few minutes.

How soon will I receive the money?

Once approved, funds are typically transferred to your bank account within 24 hours. In many cases, you can receive your money the same day if approved early in the day.

What interest rates do you charge?

Our interest rates are competitive and vary based on the loan amount, term, and your credit profile. All rates are disclosed upfront with no hidden fees. Use our calculator above for an estimate.

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes, you can pay off your loan early without any penalties. Early settlement can save you money on interest charges. Contact us to arrange early settlement.

Do you lend to people with bad credit?

We consider applications from people with various credit histories. While we do conduct credit checks, we also look at your current financial situation and ability to repay.
