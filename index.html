Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply?
You can apply directly through our mobile app or website by filling out the application form. The process takes less than 10 minutes.
How soon will I get the money?
Most applications are processed within 1 hour during business hours. Once approved, funds are typically transferred to your account on the same day.
What interest rate do you charge?
Our interest rates vary based on the loan amount, term, and your credit profile. Rates typically range from 15% to 25% per annum, in compliance with South African lending regulations.
Can I pay early without penalties?
Yes, you can pay off your loan early without any penalties. In fact, early repayment will reduce the total interest you pay.
Do you lend to blacklisted individuals?
We consider each application on a case-by-case basis. While having a negative credit record may affect your application, we look at your current income and ability to repay as well.
