How It Works

Step 1: Apply Online

Complete our secure digital form from your phone or computer. No office visit needed - apply from anywhere, anytime.

Step 2: Instant Assessment

Our advanced system and experienced team review your application in real-time, often responding within 1-2 hours.

Step 3: Get Your Cash

Once approved, your funds are transferred directly to your bank account - usually the same day or within 24 hours.

Step 4: Simple Repayment

Repay over time via convenient debit order. We also offer payment flexibility if you need to adjust your schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a loan?
You can apply directly through our mobile-optimized website by filling out the application form. The entire process takes less than 10 minutes and can be completed from your phone.
How soon will I receive the money?
Most applications are processed within 1-2 hours during business hours (8 AM - 5 PM). Once approved, funds are typically transferred to your account on the same day or within 24 hours.
What interest rates do you charge?
Our interest rates vary based on the loan amount, term, and your credit profile. Rates typically range from 15% to 25% per annum, in full compliance with South African lending regulations and NCR guidelines.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Yes, you can pay off your loan early without any penalties. In fact, early repayment will reduce the total interest you pay, saving you money.
Do you lend to people with bad credit?
We consider each application on a case-by-case basis. While having a negative credit record may affect your application, we also look at your current income and ability to repay.
Are there any upfront fees?
No, we do not charge any upfront fees. All fees and charges are clearly disclosed and only applied once your loan is approved and disbursed.
