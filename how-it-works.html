How It Works

Getting your loan is simple and fast. Follow our 4-step process to get the cash you need with no upfront fees and instant payouts.

1

Apply Online

Complete our secure digital application form from your phone or computer. The process takes just a few minutes.

  • 100% online application
  • No office visit required
  • Secure and encrypted
  • Available 24/7
2

Instant Assessment

Our advanced system and experienced team review your application in real-time, often responding within 1-2 hours.

  • Real-time processing
  • Quick credit assessment
  • Automated approval system
  • Instant notifications
3

Get Your Cash

Once approved, your funds are transferred directly to your bank account - usually the same day or within 24 hours.

  • Same day payouts
  • Direct bank transfer
  • No upfront fees
  • Instant confirmation
4

Simple Repayment

Repay over time via convenient debit order. We also offer payment flexibility if you need to adjust your schedule.

  • Automatic debit orders
  • Flexible payment dates
  • Early settlement options
  • Payment reminders

What You Need to Apply

Personal Requirements

  • South African citizen or permanent resident
  • Between 18 and 65 years old
  • Valid South African ID document
  • Proof of residence (not older than 3 months)
  • Active cellphone number
  • Valid email address

Employment Requirements

  • Employed for at least 3 months
  • Minimum monthly income of R2,500
  • Latest payslip or salary certificate
  • 3 months bank statements
  • Employment contract or letter
  • Proof of income (pensioners welcome)

Banking Requirements

  • Active South African bank account
  • Account open for at least 3 months
  • Salary paid into this account
  • Valid debit order facility
  • Bank account in your name
  • No recent returned debit orders
